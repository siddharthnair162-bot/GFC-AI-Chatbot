Summary of Findings

Apple

Revenue increased consistently over three years with modest growth. Net income surged in 2025, indicating strong profitability recovery. Liabilities dropped in 2025, improving financial stability.

Microsoft

Sustained double-digit growth across all financial metrics. Operating cash flow grew steadily, suggesting robust business efficiency. Asset base expanded rapidly, showing ongoing investments in infrastructure and AI.

Tesla

Revenue plateaued in 2024, but asset growth indicates long-term expansion. Net income declined sharply in 2024, reflecting margin pressures. Cash flow remained strong, implying resilient operations despite profit dip.

Overall Conclusion

Apple and Microsoft exhibit mature, stable growth with high profitability. Tesla shows strong scaling potential but higher volatility in profit margins.
